The Coastline

A lifestyle measured by tide and sand.

Hollywood Beach is not a postcard — it is a way of living. Mornings on the Broadwalk, afternoons on the Intracoastal, evenings on the terrace. This is the rhythm Yestheffany helps her clients find a home inside of.

01

The Broadwalk at sunrise.

Two-and-a-half miles of brick promenade between Atlantic surf and the cafés of A1A. Hollywood Beach's signature ritual — joggers, cyclists, families — and the reason long-term residents stay long-term.

02

Dinner on the Intracoastal.

From GG's Waterfront to the dockside tables at Billy's Stone Crab, the Intracoastal restaurants are where deals close, anniversaries happen, and Wednesdays feel like weekends.

03

The Hallmark of Hollywood is more than an address — it is where Yestheffany lives, works, and has represented buyers and sellers across nearly every line and view. Knowing the building is the difference between selling a unit and selling the right unit.

The amenity decks at The Hallmark, The Tides, and the towers along South Ocean Drive aren't marketing photos — they're where residents actually live. Coffee at eight, laps at noon, sunsets with neighbors.
